## Overview

Ismaila Lukman Enegi built the NUN 2015 Graduation Memorial app as a Free app. This SERVICE is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

This page is used to inform visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decides to use our Service.

---

## Information Collection and Use

**We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

The NUN 2015 Graduation Memorial app is an **offline-first memorial application** that:

- Does NOT collect any user data
- Does NOT require user registration or login
- Does NOT transmit data over the internet
- Does NOT track user behavior or analytics
- Does NOT require any permissions beyond basic app installation

### Embedded Data

All information displayed in the app (graduate profiles, photos, contact information) is:

- Embedded within the app at the time of installation
- Voluntarily provided by the 2015 graduating class members for inclusion in this memorial directory
- Read-only and cannot be modified by users
- Not transmitted, shared, or uploaded anywhere

The contact information (emails and phone numbers) shown for graduates was voluntarily provided by them for inclusion in this memorial directory and is intended to facilitate alumni networking.

## Log Data

Since the app does NOT connect to the internet and does NOT use analytics services, we do NOT collect any Log Data.

In the event of an app crash, standard Android crash reporting (managed by Google Play) may collect basic device information to help improve app stability. This is controlled by your device settings and Google Play, not by our app.

---

## Cookies

This app does NOT use cookies of any kind. All data is stored locally on your device as part of the app installation and is never transmitted elsewhere.

---

## Data Storage

### Local Storage Only

All app data is stored locally on your device:

- Graduate profiles and photos
- University official information
- Department details
- App settings (theme preference)

This data:

- Remains on your device only
- Is NOT synced to any server
- Is NOT backed up to cloud services by the app
- Is removed when you uninstall the app

### Theme Preference

The only user-generated data is your theme preference (light mode or dark mode), which is:

- Stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences
- Never transmitted anywhere
- Automatically deleted when you uninstall the app

---

## Data Security

Since we do NOT collect or transmit any personal information:

- There is no data transmission to secure
- All data remains on your device
- No servers or databases are involved
- No risk of data breaches from our app

Your device's standard Android security features protect the app data stored locally.

## Children's Privacy

This app does NOT address anyone under the age of 13, as it is a university graduation memorial app intended for:

- University alumni (18+ years old)
- Current university students
- Faculty and staff
- Academic community members

We do NOT knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Links to Other Sites

The app may display email addresses and phone numbers for graduates. Clicking on these will:

- Open your device's default email app (for email addresses)
- Open your device's default phone/dialer app (for phone numbers)

These are standard Android intents and do not involve our app collecting or transmitting any data. Your email app or phone app operates independently according to their own privacy policies.

We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the privacy practices of third-party email or phone applications on your device.
